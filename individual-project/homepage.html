College Sustainability

Why go sustainable?:

With our current world's ecological climate, it is imperative for our generation to turn around the effects of pollution we have created. Lowering one's ecological footprint as a college student can be expensive and stressful, so this site is here to help you learn about simple steps to take to lower your carbon footprint.

About the author:

I am a student at Syracuse University who has recently become aware of the amount of waste I produce. For the past two years I have been making a conscious effort to reduce my carbon footprint. I have done lots of research and am excited to teach my fellow students about the topic.
