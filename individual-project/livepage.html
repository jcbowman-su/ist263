How to Live Sustainably

Tip #1: Buy Less Waste

Shopping waste-free can be challenging, but more waste-free grocery stores are popping up everywhere.Check out this video about Lauren Singer's Package Free Shop.

Tip #2: Try Alternative Transportation Methods

Take a walk or bike to work. If it's too far or the weather is not in your favor, try public transportation like buses or subways.
